Industry glossary
Stelco
: Canadian subsidiary of Cleveland-Cliffs primarily operating in steel production, with its shipments priced on a Canadian spot basis disconnected from U.S. market pricing.
Section 232
: A U.S. trade action imposing steel import tariffs to protect domestic industry on national security grounds.
EAF (Electric Arc Furnace)
: A steelmaking furnace that uses electrical energy to melt scrap steel, as opposed to traditional blast furnace methods.
HBI (Hot-Briquetted Iron)
: Iron product used as feedstock in steelmaking processes, produced directly by reducing iron ore and briquetting it while hot.
Grain-oriented/non-oriented electrical steel
: Specialized steels used in energy and automotive sectors, with grain-oriented used mainly in transformers and non-oriented primarily in electric motors.
